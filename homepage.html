FEDRA controlled U.S.A

After the outbreak of the cordiceps fungi in 2013 and the chaos that ensued,
FEDRA (Federal disaster relief agency) would seize control of the U.S government and military,
and begin setting up Quarantine zones around the country. With less then half of the population
intact (around 150 million) and the collapse of the country, FEDRA became the last hope for most
and a large portion of those still alive would flock to the Quarantine zones. FEDRA would govern
these Quarantine zones with an iron fist until their eventual decline between 2020 and 2030.
